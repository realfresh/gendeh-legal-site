Ranjeet Gendeh

 Principal

Ranjeet completed her Bachelor of Laws with University of Wolverhampton, UK in 1994. Upon completion of her LLB, she went on to work with an Insurance Company as a legal executive. In 2000, she trained as a chambering student at a legal firm in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia which practiced in areas of general litigation, criminal and conveyancing. She was admitted to the High Court of Malaya as an Advocate and Solicitor in 2001.

In 2002, she started her law practice as a sole practitioner in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. In December 2009, Ranjeet moved to Perth and was admitted as a lawyer in Supreme Court of Western Australia on 7 February 2012.

Ranjeet has appeared and conducted numerous trials in the Subordinate Courts and High Courts and has dealt in the areas of banking, building contracts, family law, debt recovery, personal injury, employment law, wills and probate, conveyancing, property law, and criminal law. Away from the office, Ranjeet enjoys spending time with family, cooking, reading, and travelling.

Rajbir Singh

 Senior Associate

Rajbir was admitted as a lawyer to the Supreme Court of New South Wales in 2009. Rajbir has worked for small boutique law firms as well as mid-tier firms specializing in a vast range of matters, having assisted in numerous arbitrations and mediations. He also ran his own law firm in Sydney prior to moving to Perth. His areas of expertise include general litigation, property law, conveyancing, property law, personal injury, family law, contracts, and criminal law.

Rajbir has successfully settled numerous cases out of court where the clients were seriously injured following work injuries, motor accidents, or as a result of medical negligence. He has been part of a team of lawyers which successfully assisted and represented victims of asbestos exposure in receiving damages.

Rajbir has attended numerous mediations/arbitrations including attendances at the Workers Compensation Commission, Sydney, and has successfully resolved numerous matters including complex estate matters, commercial contract disputes, and personal injury matters.

Rajbir has regularly attended various Courts and tribunals, including the Magistrates Court, District Court, assisted Counsel in the Supreme Court. Away from the office Rajbir enjoys playing various sports, reading, and spending time with family.

First Home Buyers

When you are injured it is a very stressful time, both for you and your family. Whether you are injured a work, in a car accident, or hurt yourself in a public area, it can have an immediate impact on your ability to look after your loved ones.

We at Gendeh Legal can let you know exactly what are your legal entitlements and ensure you receive the financial outcome as quickly as possible. We understand that not being able to work or look after your loved ones due to your injuries can have serious consequences for both yourself and your loved ones. We know it matters!

Buyer

Separation from your partner, especially after a long relationship, can be one of the toughest periods in your life. Trying to sit down and work through all the legal issues can be overwhelming. Here at Gendeh Legal we understand the importance of the parties being able to sort out all the issues quickly, professionally, and in a cost effective manner

Whether you are seeking advice in relation to divorce, property, or children, we will guide you through in a clear manner so settlement can be achieved with least emotional and financial stress on you. We understand it matters!

Seller

Buying or selling residential property is a big decision. As your conveyancing solicitor we will advise you and guide you throughout the process up to and beyond settlement of your sale or purchase. Our services include advising on general contract law, preparing Transfer of Title, special conditions of the contract, cooling off period, liaising with your financial institution to ensure funds are available for settlement, calculating all utility adjustments, and attending settlement.

Company / Business

Your job provides you and your family ongoing financial security. When your livelihood is threatened or taken away without proper justification then you need proper legal advice as to your rights. At Gendeh Legal our professional staff can advise you on all facets of employment law, including unlawful and wrongful dismissal, unfair dismissal, equal opportunity and workplace discrimination. We will provide you with comprehensive advice on your rights and entitlements.
